## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Tesu ("the App"), an unofficial third-party Telegram client. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

**Important:** Tesu uses the Telegram API (TDLib) as its backend. We do **not** operate our own servers for message delivery or storage. All your messages, media, and communications are processed directly by Telegram's servers according to [Telegram's Privacy Policy](https://telegram.org/privacy).

## 3. What Data We Collect

### 3.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

Tesu does **not** collect, store, or transmit the following to our servers:

- ❌ Your phone number
- ❌ Your messages or chats
- ❌ Your contacts
- ❌ Your media files (photos, videos, documents)
- ❌ Your Telegram account information
- ❌ Your device ID or Android ID
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data

### 3.2 Data Handled by Telegram

When you use Tesu, your data is handled by Telegram's servers, not ours. This includes:

- Your authentication (phone number, 2FA)
- Your messages and chats
- Your media files
- Your contacts
- Your Telegram account settings

This data is governed by [Telegram's Privacy Policy](https://telegram.org/privacy), not this policy.

### 3.3 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Tesu stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

| Data | Purpose | Stored Where |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Downloaded media | Offline access | Your device storage |
| App preferences | Your settings | App's local database |
| Authentication session | Keep you logged in | Encrypted TDLib database |
| Debug logs (temporary) | Bug fixing if you report | Memory only |

**This data never leaves your device** unless you explicitly choose to share it (e.g., by sending a bug report).

### 3.4 Voluntary Bug Reports

If you choose to send a bug report:
- Device model and OS version (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy S21, Android 14")
- App version
- Sanitized debug logs (phone numbers, emails, and IDs are automatically redacted)

Bug reports are sent via **your email client** directly to our support email. We do not have access to your data unless you explicitly send it.

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Telegram API (TDLib)

Tesu uses the official Telegram Database Library (TDLib) to connect to Telegram's servers. Your communication data is processed by Telegram according to their policies:

- [Telegram Privacy Policy](https://telegram.org/privacy)
- [Telegram Terms of Service](https://telegram.org/tos)

### 5.2 Google Play Services

If you installed Tesu from Google Play Store:
- **In-App Updates:** Google Play may transmit app version information to check for updates
- **Play Store Reviews:** If you leave a review, it's governed by Google's policies
- **Google AdMob:** We display non-personalized ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your Advertising ID to serve relevant ads. No personal data is shared with advertisers.

We do not receive any personal data from Google Play Services.

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Local Data Protection

- Authentication data is encrypted by TDLib using industry-standard encryption
- Downloaded files are stored in standard storage locations
- We do not implement additional tracking or monitoring

### 6.2 No Server-Side Storage

Since we do not operate servers, there is no risk of server-side data breaches from our end. Your Telegram data's security is managed by Telegram.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 7.1 Access and Control

- **View your data:** All your data is on your device and in your Telegram account
- **Delete your data:** Uninstall the app to remove all local data

### 7.2 Your Rights Under Law

Since we do not collect your personal data, most data protection rights (access, correction, deletion) apply to:
- **Telegram data:** Contact Telegram directly
